Hello my name is Vicuko and this is my personal journey through the world!

It all began 2 years ago. At that time I was working as a consultant for a big international company 24/7. My job required traveling to many different places and working with many people from varied cultures. At first I really liked the idea of traveling and working with other people, but in the long term it became bulky and the weight began to increase since I had less and less time for myself and my beloved ones, what's even more, spending time with my family was seen as something negative by my boss... something I never quite understood.

After that, I began to think of ways to leave my current lifestyle and embark in a new adventure with my partner in life.And so we went on, into the undiscovered...
